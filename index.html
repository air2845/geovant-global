How it works

Ground truth data,
not scraped data

Every location in our database was physically visited, photographed, and reviewed by a human before publication.

1
Collect
Local ambassadors physically visit each location. GPS is captured on-site. A photo of the entrance is required. Details are recorded in our scout app.
2
Review
Every submission is reviewed by a human administrator before approval. Only verified data is published.
3
Deliver
Verified locations are immediately available via REST API. CSV and GeoJSON exports available on demand.
