How we build it

Ground truth, not
scraped guesses

Every location passes through six steps before it reaches the API. No shortcuts, no satellite-only inference.

01
📍
Physical Visit
A local ambassador physically travels to the location. No remote mapping — boots on the ground, every time.
02
📷
Photo + GPS
A photo of the entrance and precise on-site GPS coordinates are captured in our scout app, together.
03
🧭
Landmark Directions
Human-readable directions — "beside the blue mosque" — because coordinates alone don't navigate informal streets.
04
✅
Human Review
Every submission is checked by a human administrator. Bad GPS, blurry photos and duplicates are rejected before publishing.
05
🔄
Continuous Updates
Ambassadors re-verify older locations on a rolling schedule, so the database reflects the ground as it changes.
06
⚡
Live API
Verified locations are instantly available over a REST API — JSON and GeoJSON, filterable by category, city and radius.
